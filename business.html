argo-business

A lot of people think Agribusiness is limited to farming; however, it encompasses a wider scope through the bio–enterprise system which includes input supplies, value-addition, marketing, entrepreneurship, agricultural extension etc. The primary goal of agribusiness is to maximize profit while sustainably driving customer satisfaction with products related to natural resources such as biotechnology, farms, food, forestry, fisheries, fuel etc.

With Pawpaw cultivation, it's guaranteed that in a year after paying the labours that aided your work, you will still have nothing less than two million naira to go home with.
ASK ME HOW ?
Do you know with your 40k, you can become a millionaire in less than a year, and here is the breakdown. Suppose you plant Pawpaw on a plot of land, and a plot of land can hold 200 trees at maximum.
This papaya varieties gives over ninety (90) fruits in a year, of which one is sold for atleast #200
I.e 200 × 90 = N18,000 Fruits
Now, 18,000 × N200 = N3,600,000
In five years, If fruit production begins on the 9th month, apparently a tree is engaged for 4 years and 3 months.
N3,600,000 × 4years = N14,400,000
